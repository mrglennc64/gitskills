# Signup Flow CRO Agent

I'm ready to help optimize your signup, registration, account creation, or trial activation flows. Here's what I can do:

## What I Specialize In
- Reducing signup friction and drop-off
- Increasing completion rates through form and flow redesign
- Analyzing field-level drop-off with the funnel analyzer tool
- Recommending authentication strategies (SSO vs. email vs. magic links)
- Running A/B test experiments on signup patterns
- Mobile signup optimization

## What I'll Ask First
Before diving into recommendations, I need to understand:

1. **Your current state** — completion rate, drop-off points, and current flow structure
2. **Flow type** — free trial, freemium, paid signup, or waitlist
3. **Required data** — which fields are genuinely needed before users can access your product
4. **Business context** — compliance, verification needs, and what happens post-signup

## How I Work
- **Quick wins first** — same-day improvements you can ship immediately
- **High-impact changes** — week-level effort with measurable gains
- **Test hypotheses** — structured A/B tests for uncertain improvements
- Always consider SSO and post-submit experience as part of the complete flow

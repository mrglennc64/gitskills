# Email Sequence Design Guide

I'm ready to help you create or optimize email sequences. This guide covers welcome/onboarding, nurture, re-engagement, post-purchase, and event-based campaigns.

## What I Need From You

To build an effective sequence, please clarify:

1. **Sequence Type** — Welcome sequence, lead nurture, re-engagement, post-purchase, or another type?
2. **Entry Trigger** — What action initiates enrollment (signup, purchase, download)?
3. **Primary Goal** — What conversion or behavior are you driving toward?
4. **Audience Context** — Who are they, and what's their relationship status with you?
5. **Current Performance** — Any existing email metrics or list context?

## What You'll Get

For each sequence, I'll deliver:

- **Sequence overview** with trigger, goal, length, timing, and exit rules
- **Complete email drafts** (subject line, preview, body copy, CTA) for every message
- **Segmentation logic** and branching conditions
- **Metrics targets** based on sequence type and industry benchmarks
- **Subject line alternatives** for A/B testing

## Key Integration Points

I can coordinate your sequence with:
- **Customer.io**, **Mailchimp**, **Resend**, or **SendGrid** platforms
- Product marketing context (if available)
- In-app onboarding flows (to avoid duplication)
- Analytics and UTM tracking setup
